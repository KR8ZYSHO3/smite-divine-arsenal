SMITE 2 Divine Arsenal - How It Works

The AI Optimizer Algorithm

Our Advanced Build Optimizer uses a sophisticated multi-stage algorithm that considers dozens of factors to create optimal builds:

Step 1: God Analysis

Analyzes the selected god's damage type (Physical/Magical), abilities, and natural role fit to determine core item categories.

Step 2: Role-Based Selection

Applies role-specific item pools and priorities. Solo prioritizes survivability, Mid focuses on burst damage, Carry emphasizes sustained damage, etc.

Step 3: Meta Analysis

Evaluates each item's current meta rating (S/A/B/C tier) based on pick rates, win rates, and professional play trends.

Step 4: Synergy Detection

Identifies powerful item combinations and ensures the build has good synergy between items (e.g., critical chance + critical damage).

Step 5: Power Progression

Optimizes the build order for smooth power spikes throughout early, mid, and late game phases.
